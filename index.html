Guides

Here at BrickMii, we present humorous guides on "bricking" your Wii. Please remember, these methods are not to be taken seriously and should not be attempted.

Method 1: The Homebrew Channel & KoreanKii (003 Brick)

Note: This method is presented for entertainment purposes only and should not be attempted. It may result in rendering your Wii console unusable.

You'll need: a Nintendo Wii with HBC installed, and an SD card with the Homebrew Browser installed on it.


Step 1. Put the SD card into your Wii.

Step 2. Turn your Wii on.

Step 3. Open the Homebrew Browser on your Wii.

Step 4. Install KoreanKii from the Homebrew Browser. (note: it may take a bit of searching to find KoreanKii in the list)

Step 5. Launch KoreanKii from the Homebrew Channel.

A menu will show asking you to press + to add the Korean key and - to remove the Korean key.

Step 6. Press + to add the Korean key. When it asks you if you are sure and want to take the risks, press YES. Note that this may render your console unusable.

Step 7. Enjoy your "bricked" console!

Method 2: The Literal Brick.

Note: This method is presented for entertainment purposes only and should not be attempted. It may result in causing severe damage to your Wii console.

You'll need: a Nintendo Wii, and a brick.


Step 1. Turn your Wii off if it is already on. I recommend taking it outside before performing this method.

Step 2. Take your brick and drop it onto your Wii from a high height. You can make this more effective by slamming the brick onto the Wii.

Step 3. Enjoy your "bricked" console!
