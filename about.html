About BrickMii

We are the world's first website dedicated to the humorous exploration of "bricking" the Nintendo Wii.

BrickMii was born out of a whimsical desire to create a platform where users could explore the absurdity of intentionally rendering their Wii consoles unusable. While the idea may seem impractical and humorous, we take pride in being pioneers in this unique niche.

It's important to note that BrickMii is purely a comedic venture, and we do not condone or endorse any illegal activities, including damaging or tampering with Wii consoles. Our aim is to entertain and amuse, not to promote harmful behaviors.

About Us

BrickMii was created as a joke website just for the developer (Arslaan Pathan, @RealArslaanYT on YouTube) to learn web development.

It was never meant to be a proper site, and it never really will. Though, Arslaan Pathan, the developer of this site, does have plans to upgrade his current site with his newfound knowledge.
